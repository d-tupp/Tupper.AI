TupperAI: The Tupper Family Tree

This website provides the full family tree history of the Tupper Family, focusing on the line from Sir Charles Tupper, 6th Prime Minister of Canada. Note that Sir Charles Tupper is a descendant of Richard Warren (c. 1578–1628), a Mayflower passenger.

AI Features

The AI query feature is under development. In future updates, you'll be able to ask natural language questions about the family tree (e.g., "Who are the children of Sir Charles Tupper?") using an integrated AI model.
